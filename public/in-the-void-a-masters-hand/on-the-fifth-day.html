On the Fifth Day

No need to hide,
you’re hurting inside,
sobbing, like rolling thunder,
uncovered, shivering, at night.

I’m sorry,
if I was out-of-sight,
like a shadow at night,
haunting you, creating anguish;
but now,

Let’s flush the pain
falling from your eyes,
we’ll stitch the wounds of time,
So please,

Feel my love,
no more sorrows,
you’re a Cosmic Rose,
starting, let’s propose,
blooming in the morrow.

Let’s, you and me,
repaint your canvas
wild and free,
colors vibrant and bright
I’ll guide your brushstrokes
leaving trails of divine delight.

So blink back the tears
give me your fears,
remember,
my cosmic rose,
My love for you,
It grows.

I breathed life into you,
your soul’s first breath,
now gasping from sorrow,
you are listening to Me,
ready to hear My plea–

Come Back To Me.

From
